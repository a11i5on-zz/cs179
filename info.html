Instruction Manual

Feel free to return to this page at any point by clicking the ⓘ icon at the top right of every page.

  1. First, use our "Train" portal to improve your skills. There are both training materials and mock tests.
  2. Upon completion of the training module or achieving proficiency in a particular skill, schedule a test (can be accessed through the "More Options" dropdown) at your registered shelter.
  3. If you pass the certification test, the shelter will certify you electronically, and you will be able to work jobs listed on the "Work" pages that require your certified skill.
  4. Complete as many jobs as needed; when you desire to be paid, navigate to the main page and click "Get Paid" to initiate a notification to the shelter, where you can pick up your payment in cash.
